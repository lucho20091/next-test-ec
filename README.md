# TechStore

Welcome to TechStore, your go-to online destination for premium electronics and gadgets! This application provides a seamless e-commerce experience for browsing products, managing your cart, placing orders, and tracking shipments.

## About TechStore

TechStore is built to offer a modern and efficient online shopping experience. We are committed to providing high-quality tech products, excellent customer service, and a secure platform for all your electronic needs.

## Features

*   **Product Catalog**: Browse a wide range of electronics and gadgets with detailed descriptions and images.
*   **Shopping Cart**: Add products to your cart, adjust quantities, and manage your selections before checkout.
*   **Secure Checkout**: A streamlined checkout process with integrated payment options (PayPal and dummy payment for testing).
*   **Order Management**: View your order history, track shipping statuses, and review past purchases.
*   **User Authentication**: Secure user sign-in and sign-out powered by Stackframe.
*   **Admin Panel**: (For authorized users) Manage products, view all orders, and oversee user accounts.
*   **Responsive Design**: Enjoy a consistent and user-friendly experience across various devices.

## Technologies Used

TechStore is built with a robust and modern tech stack to ensure performance, scalability, and maintainability:

*   **Next.js**: For server-side rendering, routing, and API routes.
*   **React**: For building interactive user interfaces.
*   **Prisma**: As the ORM for efficient and type-safe database interactions.
*   **Tailwind CSS & shadcn/ui**: For a utility-first approach to styling and accessible UI components.
*   **Stackframe**: For secure authentication and user management.
*   **Cloudinary**: For image storage and delivery.
*   **PayPal React SDK**: For payment processing.
*   **React Hot Toast**: For user notifications.
*   **Lucide React**: For a rich set of icons.

## Usage

### Browsing Products
Navigate to the homepage to see all available products. Click on any product to view its detailed page, including description, price, and stock availability.

### Shopping and Checkout
Add desired products to your cart. You can adjust quantities or remove items from the cart page. Proceed to checkout to enter shipping information and complete your purchase.

### My Orders
Once an order is placed, you can view its details and current shipping status in the "Orders" section.

### Admin Access
If you have administrator privileges, you can access the `/admin` routes to manage products, orders, and users.
